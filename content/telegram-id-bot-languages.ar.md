## FAQ

### Kayfa aghayyir lughat al-bot?
Ghayyir lughat Telegram app fi Settings, Language. Al-bot yastakhdimuha. La miftah lugha fi al-bot.

### Ma idha lughati ghayr mad'uma?
Al-bot yarji' ila al-Injiliziyya. Jami' al-wizafat ta'mal nafsaha.

### Hal al-bot yad'um lughat RTL?
Na'am. Al-'Arabiyya wa-l-Farsiyya wa-l-Urdu mad'uma. Telegram yudabbir RTL rendering.

### Hal yumkinuni talab lugha jadida?
Al-bot yad'um 16 lugha. Ziyadat al-mazid ta'umu 'ala al-mudabbirin. Ruju' al-Injiliziyya yadman anna al-jami' yastati'u al-istikhdam.

### Hal tukhazzan al-lugha aw tursal ila makan akhar?
La. Al-bot yaqra' language_code min kull tahdith wayastakhdimuhu faqat li-ikhtiyar response strings. La tukhazzan wa-la tusharak.
