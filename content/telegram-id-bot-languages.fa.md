## FAQ

### Chetor zaban-e robot ra avaz konam?
Zaban-e app-e Telegram ra dar Settings, Language taghir dahid. Robot az an estefade mikonad. Kelid-e zaban dar robot nist.

### Agar zaban-e man poshtibani nashode?
Robot be English barmigardad. Hame gabeliat-ha yeksan kar mikonand.

### Aya robot zaban-ha-ye RTL ra poshtibani mikonad?
Bale. Arabi, Farsi va Urdu poshtibani mishavand. Telegram render-e RTL ra anjam midahad.

### Mitavanam zaban-e jadid darkhast konam?
Robot 16 zaban poshtibani mikonad. Ezafe kardan-e bishtar be negahdaran vabaste ast. Bargasht be English zamemat mikonad hame betavanand estefade konand.

### Aya zaban zakhire ya jaye digar ferestade mishavad?
Kheir. Robot language_code ra az har update mikhanad va faghat baraye entekhab-e response string-ha estefade mikonad. Zakhire ya share nemishavad.
